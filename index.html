Day 1 - Shaliputri Devi

Color: Yellow

Avatar:Daughter of Himalayas, embodiment of strength

Special:Worshipped for health and Prosperity

Day 2 - Brahmacharini Devi

Color:Green

Avatar:Goddess of penance and meditation

Special:Brings wisdom, devotion, and strength of mind

Day 3 - Chandraghanta Devi

Color:Grey

Avatar:Half-moon on forehead, rides a tiger

Special:Removes fear, grants courage and bravery

Day 4 - Kushmanda Devi

Color:Orange

Avatar:Creator of the universe, radiates energy like the sun

Special:Blesses with energy, health, and prosperity

Day 5 - Skandamata Devi

Color:White

Avatar:Mother of Lord Skanda, rides a lion with child in lap

Special:Symbol of motherhood, blesses with wisdom and strength

Color:Red

Avatar:Warrior Goddess born to Rishi katyayan, rides a lion

Special:Brings courage,love, and protection from evil

Color:Royal Blue

Avatar:Fierce form of Durga, destroys demons and evil

Special:Removes negativity, fears, and obstacles

Day 8 - Mahagauri Devi

Color:Pink

Avatar:Calm, fair form of parvati, symbol of purity

Special:Brings peace, patience, and prosperity

day 9 - Siddhidatri devi
